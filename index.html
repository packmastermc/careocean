Welcome to CareOcean.org

CareOcean shines as a guiding force in the realm of ocean conservation, its inception spearheaded by the esteemed researcher Professor Xiutang Yuan. With a steadfast commitment to safeguarding our planet's aquatic treasures, our non-profit foundation seeks to illuminate the importance of ocean protection and its profound impact on marine biology. Through educational initiatives and collaborative partnerships, we endeavor to empower communities to become proactive stewards of our oceans. At the heart of our mission lies a deep-rooted belief in the interconnectedness of all life forms and ecosystems, driving us to advocate for sustainable practices and policies that ensure the longevity of our marine environments. Join us as we chart a course towards a brighter future for our oceans and all who rely upon them for sustenance.

Founder Professor Yuan

Mr. Yuan is a Researcher (Professor) currently serving for the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).

Yuan specializes in marine biology, leveraging his expertise to advance scientific knowledge and drive innovation in this field. His books, research theses, and conference presentations are widely acclaimed and frequently referenced within academic circles, serving as foundational resources for scholars worldwide.
