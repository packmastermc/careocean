Professor Yuan

Mr. Yuan is a Researcher (Professor) currently serving for the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). In parallel with esteemed institutions like the US National Academy of Sciences (NAS), CNRS, and RAS, the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) stands as one of the foremost renowned national research organizations worldwide. Before CAS, Professor Yuan served as a top researcher for State Oceanic Administration of China, a branch affiliated to Chinaâ€™s central government.

Professor Yuan specializes in marine biology, leveraging his expertise to advance scientific knowledge and drive innovation in this field. His books, research theses, and conference presentations are widely acclaimed and frequently referenced within academic circles, serving as foundational resources for scholars worldwide. Additionally, he holds the esteemed position as the foremost authority in establishing ocean environmental quality standards, a testament to his unparalleled expertise and influence in the field.

Moreover, his contributions extend beyond academia. Collaborating with research organizations and companies worldwide, he champions the cause of ocean conservation, spearheading initiatives to raise awareness and enhance quality standards. His dedication to promoting environmental stewardship and his ability to effect positive change make him a driving force in the global effort to safeguard our marine ecosystems.

Professor Yuan is Director or Standing Committee Member of the following organizations: Marine Standardization Subcommittee of the China Pacific Society, National Union of Modern Marine Ranching Science and Technology Innovation and Chinese Society for Oceanology and Limnology.
